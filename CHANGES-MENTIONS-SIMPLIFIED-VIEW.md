# Mentions — simplified views

- Removed the separate **New** tab.
- Mentions now have only **All** and **Hidden** views.
- In **All**, unacknowledged threads are grouped at the top under **New**.
- Acknowledged threads follow under **Earlier**.
- The All tab displays the total visible count and a small new-mention count.
- Existing badge, seen, hide, restore and My To-Do behaviour is unchanged.
