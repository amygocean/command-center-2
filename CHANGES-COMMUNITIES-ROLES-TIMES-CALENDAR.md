# Communities: roles, send times and responsive calendar

- Added **Bar / Deli** as a Community role and Asana section target.
- Added optional send times stored as Asana `due_at` values. The picker supports five-minute increments and manual times.
- Added favourite/unfavourite time controls with defaults of **10:00, 15:00 and 18:00**.
- Timed messages keep their time when dragged to a different date.
- Send date and time can also be edited later from the task drawer.
- Communities calendar now renders every message in its day cell, sorts by time, and grows each week row to fit the busiest day.
- The calendar keeps readable widths on small screens and scrolls horizontally rather than compressing message cards.
- Times appear in the Communities calendar, upcoming list, preview and main calendar pills.
